A comprehensive resource for graduate students in the Communication and Culture program at Toronto Metropolitan University and York University.

What is the Pirate Guide?

The Pirate Guide to ComCult is a collaboratively-edited resource created by and for graduate students navigating the Communication and Culture program. It contains practical information, tips, and guidance that goes beyond what you'll find in official program documentation.

How to Use This Guide

Browse the sections in the sidebar to find information on:

  • Getting Started – Introduction and important links
  • Program Requirements – Coursework, supervisors, committees, exams, and dissertations
  • Academic Life – Publishing, conferences, and presentations
  • Finances & Employment – Money matters, jobs, and grants
  • Living Well – Healthcare, food, and housing resources
  • Intersections – Resources for students with specific experiences and identities

Contributing

This guide is collaboratively maintained. You can:

  • Edit any page – Click "Edit this page on GitHub" at the bottom of any section
  • Add a new section – Create a new markdown file in the _wiki folder
  • Discuss changes – Use the comments section on any page or join our discussions

The Pirate Guide is maintained by the ComCult graduate student community. All contributions welcome.

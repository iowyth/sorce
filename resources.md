## Research-Creation Reading List

A bibliography of key texts on practice-based research, artistic inquiry, and creative methodologies. Organized by theme and roughly chronological within each section.

### Foundational Texts

- McNiff, Shaun. *Art-Based Research*. Jessica Kingsley Publishers, 1998.

- Haseman, Brad. "A Manifesto for Performative Research." *Media International Australia*, vol. 118, no. 1, 2006.

- Smith, Hazel, and Roger T. Dean, eds. *Practice-led Research, Research-led Practice in the Creative Arts*. Edinburgh University Press, 2009.

- Barrett, Estelle, and Barbara Bolt. *Practice as Research: Approaches to Creative Arts Enquiry*. I.B. Tauris, 2010.

- Sullivan, Graeme. *Art Practice as Research: Inquiry in Visual Arts*. 2nd ed., SAGE, 2010.

- Chapman, Owen, and Kim Sawchuk. "Research-Creation: Intervention, Analysis and 'Family Resemblances.'" *Canadian Journal of Communication*, vol. 37, no. 1, 2012.

- Nelson, Robin. *Practice as Research in the Arts: Principles, Protocols, Pedagogies, Resistances*. Palgrave Macmillan, 2013.

- Chapman, Owen, and Kim Sawchuk. "Creation-as-Research: Critical Making in Complex Environments." *RACAR*, vol. 40, no. 1, 2015.

- Leavy, Patricia. *Method Meets Art: Arts-Based Research Practice*. 3rd ed., Guilford Press, 2020.

- Leavy, Patricia, ed. *Handbook of Arts-Based Research*. 2nd ed., Guilford Press, 2025.

### Research-Creation Theory

- Manning, Erin, and Brian Massumi. *Thought in the Act: Passages in the Ecology of Experience*. University of Minnesota Press, 2014.

- Manning, Erin. "Ten Propositions for Research-Creation." In *Collaboration in Performance Practice*, edited by Noyale Colin and Stefanie Sachsenmaier, 133–141. Palgrave Macmillan, 2016.

- Manning, Erin. *The Minor Gesture*. Duke University Press, 2016.

- Loveless, Natalie. *How to Make Art at the End of the World: A Manifesto for Research-Creation*. Duke University Press, 2019.

- Loveless, Natalie, ed. *Knowings and Knots: Methodologies and Ecologies in Research-Creation*. University of Alberta Press, 2019.

- Truman, Sarah E. "The Intimacies of Doing Research-Creation: Sarah E. Truman in Conversation with Natalie Loveless, Erin Manning, Natasha Myers, and Stephanie Springgay." In *Knowings and Knots*, edited by Natalie Loveless. University of Alberta Press, 2019.

- Manning, Erin. *For a Pragmatics of the Useless*. Duke University Press, 2020.

- Springgay, Stephanie. *Feltness: Research-Creation, Socially Engaged Art, and Affective Pedagogies*. Duke University Press, 2022.

### Critical Making & Media

- Ratto, Matt. "Critical Making: Conceptual and Material Studies in Technology and Social Life." *The Information Society*, vol. 27, no. 4, 2011.

- Hertz, Garnet, and Jussi Parikka. "Zombie Media: Circuit Bending Media Archaeology into an Art Method." *Leonardo*, vol. 45, no. 5, 2012.

- Dunne, Anthony, and Fiona Raby. *Speculative Everything: Design, Fiction, and Social Dreaming*. MIT Press, 2013.

- McNiff, Shaun, ed. *Art as Research: Opportunities and Challenges*. Intellect, 2013.

- Hertz, Garnet. *Conversations in Critical Making*. CTheory Books, 2015.

### A/r/tography & Walking Methodologies

- Springgay, Stephanie, Rita L. Irwin, and Sylvia Wilson Kind. "A/r/tography as Living Inquiry Through Art and Text." *Qualitative Inquiry*, vol. 11, no. 6, 2005, pp. 897–912.

- Springgay, Stephanie, Rita L. Irwin, Carl Leggo, and Peter Gouzouasis, eds. *Being with A/r/tography*. Sense Publishers, 2008.

- Springgay, Stephanie, and Sarah E. Truman. *Walking Methodologies in a More-than-human World: WalkingLab*. Routledge, 2018.

- Springgay, S., and Z. Zaliwska. "Diagrams and Cuts: A Materialist Approach to Research-Creation." *Cultural Studies ↔ Critical Methodologies*, vol. 15, no. 2, 2015.

### Methodology & Ethics

- Vaughan, Kathleen. "Pieced Together: Collage as an Artist's Method for Interdisciplinary Research." *International Journal of Qualitative Methods*, vol. 4, no. 1, 2005.

- Marshall, Julia. "Image as Insight: Visual Images in Practice-Based Research." *Studies in Art Education*, vol. 49, no. 1, 2007.

- Bolt, Barbara, and Robert Vincs. "Straw Godzilla: Engaging the Academy and Research Ethics in Artistic Research Projects." *Educational Philosophy and Theory*, vol. 47, no. 12, 2015.

- Skains, R. Lyle. *Designing and Conducting Practice-Based Research Projects: A Practical Guide for Arts Student Researchers*. Intellect, 2022.

### Indigenous, Decolonial & Embodied Approaches

- Smith, Linda Tuhiwai. *Decolonizing Methodologies: Research and Indigenous Peoples*. 3rd ed., Zed Books, 2021. First published 1999.

- Wilson, Shawn. *Research Is Ceremony: Indigenous Research Methods*. Fernwood Publishing, 2008.

- Kovach, Margaret. *Indigenous Methodologies: Characteristics, Conversations and Contexts*. 2nd ed., University of Toronto Press, 2021. First published 2009.

- Absolon, Kathleen E. (Minogiizhigokwe). *Kaandossiwin: How We Come to Know: Indigenous Re-Search Methodologies*. 2nd ed., Fernwood Publishing, 2022. First published 2011.

- Papadopoulos, Dimitris. *Experimental Practice: Technoscience, Alterontologies, and More-than-Social Movements*. Duke University Press, 2018.

- Martin, K. "Embodiment in an Indigenous Lit Classroom: Why I'm Over Discussion but Can't Get Enough of Research-Creation." *Studies in American Indian Literatures*, vol. 34, no. 1, 2022.

### Collaborative Practice

- Salter, Christopher L., and Sha Xin Wei. "Sponge: A Case Study in Practice-Based Collaborative Art Research." *Proceedings of the 5th Conference on Creativity & Cognition*, ACM Press, 2005.

- Thayne, Martyn, and Andrew West. "'Doing' Media Studies: The Media Lab as Entangled Media Praxis." *Convergence*, 2019.

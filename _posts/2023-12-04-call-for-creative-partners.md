A community invitation seeking collaborative event planners interested in non-hierarchical organizational dynamics within the framework of social justice.

## Who We Are

SORCE is a research-creation collective at T'karonto Metropolitan University and York University. We work through open-ended, creative, empowering methods for pursuing justice and structural change.

## What We're Looking For

We're seeking creative partners who are interested in:

- Non-hierarchical organizational dynamics
- Social justice frameworks
- Collaborative event planning
- Research-creation methodologies

## How to Get Involved

If you're a masters student, doctoral candidate, artist, or community ally interested in joining our work, reach out through our contact channels.

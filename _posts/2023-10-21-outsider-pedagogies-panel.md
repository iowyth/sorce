A panel discussion featuring SORCE members Miranda McKee and iowyth hezel ulthiin, with contributions from Alexa Vachon.

## Event Details

**Date:** October 26th, 12:30–1:30 pm EDT
**Format:** Online (free)

## Topic

"Research-Creation for Social Change" — exploring research-creation as methodology combining artistic and academic praxis.

## Discussion Areas

- Counter-mapping as research method
- Participatory theatre and embodied inquiry
- Improv spaces and emergent knowledge
- Photography for climate activism

## Questions

How does creative practice function as rigorous research? What methodological and ethical considerations arise when art becomes scholarship? How can research-creation contribute to social justice movements?
